Energy Usage Prediction

Millions of dollars are at stake for Texas energy retailers. Due to the deregulated energy market, being able to accurately forecast future energy usage can have a large impact on a retailer's profit.

For Baylor's Spring 2016 Capstone Design project, my team and I were responsible for creating a system that predicts future energy load. The slides for our final presentation are available here.
